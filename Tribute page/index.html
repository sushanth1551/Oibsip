Biography

Prabhas Raju Uppalapati, known mononymously as Prabhas, is an Indian film actor who works primarily in Telugu cinema. He was born on October 23, 1979, in Chennai, India. Prabhas gained immense popularity for his role as Baahubali in the epic film series "Baahubali: The Beginning" and "Baahubali: The Conclusion," which became one of the highest-grossing Indian films of all time.

He made his acting debut in 2002 with the film "Eshwar" and has since appeared in several successful films, including "Mirchi," "Saaho," and "Radhe Shyam." Prabhas is known for his dedication to his roles, often undergoing significant physical transformations for his characters. He has a massive fan following and is regarded as one of the leading actors in Indian cinema.
